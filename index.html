Map of Kentucky's Poverty Index and Awarded Community Development Block Grants


Link to high resolution map

Authored by: Emily Rodes

This map was made using QGIS3. Data was made available by Louisville (LOJIC) Open Geospatial Data. The map indicates Kentucky's Low-poverty index by county, as well as the awarded Community Development Block Grants for the state. The map indicates that most of the block grants are located within urban areas. Though this is the purpose of the grants, it also shows a lack of grants in rural parts of Kentucky, particularly Eastern Kentucky. Louisville's Open GeoSpatial Data website.
